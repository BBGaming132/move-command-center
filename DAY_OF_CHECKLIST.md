# Secure move-day checklist

## The evening before

1. Confirm every phone has successfully signed in online.
2. Confirm each phone shows the correct username and a distinct device ID.
3. Confirm `App cached`, `Cloud synced`, and 388 inventory pieces.
4. Fully close and reopen the installed PWA on each phone.
5. Turn on Airplane Mode and verify the inventory, search, edits, and backup export still work.
6. Restore connectivity and confirm the test edit synchronizes to another phone.
7. Undo every test receiving action.
8. Export one final planning backup.
9. Charge all phones and power banks.

## Truck arrival

1. Open the installed PWA, not a private/incognito browser tab.
2. Do not sign out, clear Safari data, or delete the Home Screen app.
3. Confirm the correct device name before recording events.
4. Keep one phone with the inventory lead and use the other phones for planning, exceptions, or verification.

## During unloading

1. Search the printed item number or any spoken keyword.
2. Call out the destination code before the mover leaves the check-in point.
3. Mark received only after the item is physically accounted for.
4. Log an issue for damage, missing pieces, mismatches, or uncertain placement.
5. Continue working offline if service drops; the app will queue changes locally.
6. Do not reload for an app update during active unloading.

## Before the truck leaves

1. Filter to Remaining and investigate every result.
2. Review all issue records with the crew lead.
3. Confirm every physical crate is empty by its current label.
4. Check the truck and staging areas.
5. Reconnect all devices and wait for `Cloud synced`.
6. Export a final backup before signing delivery completion.
